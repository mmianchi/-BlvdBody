ultrasonics cavitation

Ultrasonic Cavitation (ultrasound liposuction) is a new, noninvasive, & revolutionary fat removal technology. Cavitation is a natural phenomenon based on low frequency ultrasound. The ultrasound field creates bubbles in the liquid that surround the fat cells, which gradually grow, and implode. As the membrances of fat cells do no not have the structural capacity to withstand the vibrations, the effect of cavitation easilly breaks them, While sparing the vascular, nervous and muscular tissue.
